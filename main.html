No More Collection Of Unwanted Gifts

We make it easy for you to to collate, organize and keep track of the things you really want. Perhaps use this to store bucket type wish-list that keeps your dreams alive. We also make it easy to share your list with friends and family. What’s on your wish-list? What do you do if you’re trying to live sustainably or minimally but your family or friends love giving presents? This can be a difficult topic and conversation to have. Gift giving a certain way can become a tradition for families and people generally aren’t happy with change – especially with traditions. You could all use a wish-list. On this site, everyone lists things they’d like, you can then share it with a group and mark things that are purchased so there won’t be duplicates. This way people can ask for things they "actually want" and it makes shopping easier for everyone.
